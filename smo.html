Social Media Optimization (SMO)

I have been familiar with SEO (Search Engine Optimization), but this is the first time I have heard about Social Media Optimization (SMO). Instead of growing your website on Search Engine Results Pages, Social Media Optimization is about strategically creating, building and maximizing your social media plan to connect with your target audience.

There are 4 main benefits of SMO. They strengthen your brand, generate leads, get more visibility online, and connect with your audience. SMO won’t affect your search engine ranking directly, but it is the catalyst to give you a strong base. “One survey found 50% of companies that aren’t succeeding with SEO aren’t integrating social media marketing.” It means if you want to get a high rank in SERPs, you should balance SEO and SMO. In other words, make your brand becoming a social business.

Here are some ways to help you to improve your SMO.

  • Pick the right strategy.
  • Do keyword research.
  • Optimize social profile. Make it clear and exactly.
  • Content is king. Optimize it.
  • Optimize the content schedule. Need to know the best time to publish your content.
  • Tracking everything. If you don't measure it you can't manage it.
